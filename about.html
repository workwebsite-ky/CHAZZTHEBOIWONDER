The Story

Mentorship, friendship, and countless hours in the studio — this is how the movement was built.

Boi Wonder Entertainment

Chazz The Boi Wonder

Out of North Augusta, South Carolina, Chazz The Boi Wonder is carving his own lane in Southern Soul — bringing fresh ideas, sharp production concepts, and a relentless work ethic to a genre he loves.

While many artists chase trends, Chazz is focused on building something that stands the test of time. His latest single, “Make It Rain (Rain Dance),” turned into a viral movement in under two weeks — and he’s just getting started.

The Mission

Make real Southern Soul that moves people — records with heart, energy, and staying power, made to ride to from Augusta to the world.

The Vision

Build a catalog and a movement, not a moment — bridging generations of Southern Soul while bringing new fans to the culture.

The Formula

Two generations. One sound.
